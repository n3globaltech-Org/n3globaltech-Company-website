FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on scope and complexity. A simple web app might take 4–8 weeks, while an enterprise platform could take 6–12 months. We provide detailed timelines during the scoping phase.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We love working with startups and offer flexible engagement models. We can help with MVP development, technical co-founding roles, or full product teams.

What technologies do you specialize in?

Our stack covers React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, Flutter, AWS, GCP, Azure, PostgreSQL, and many more. We always choose the best tool for your specific use case.

Can we start with a small pilot project?

Yes! A pilot or proof-of-concept is often a great way for us to work together, demonstrate our capabilities, and establish trust before committing to a larger engagement.

Do you provide support after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, monitoring, updates, and feature development. We can structure a retainer agreement to ensure your product stays healthy and evolving.

How does pricing work?

We offer fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes, and time-and-materials for flexible engagements. Pricing depends on team size, timeline, and technology requirements.
