How We Work

Engagement Models

Pick the way of working that fits your project. We're flexible across all three, and most clients shift between them as their needs evolve.

Fixed-price project

Best for clearly scoped builds with a defined deliverable. We share milestones, timeline, and a flat price upfront after a paid discovery call.

For: websites, MVPs, single platforms

Dedicated team

Best for ongoing product work, scaling SaaS, or evolving roadmaps. A small team works as part of yours on a monthly retainer with regular sprints.

For: SaaS scale-up, long-term partners

Hourly consulting

Best for advisory, code reviews, or short engagements where the scope can't be fixed in advance. Tracked weekly, billed monthly, no minimum commitment.

For: tech reviews, expert input, fixes

Not sure which fits? Most projects start with a paid discovery call where we map out the right model together. Tell us about your project and we'll suggest the cleanest path.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how we work and the services we provide.

Do you only provide services, or do you build your own products?

We are a hybrid company. While we build custom software and SaaS platforms for our clients, we also engineer and operate our own proprietary SaaS products like N3 ServiceBook and N3 FitBook. This dual approach ensures we stay sharp on production-level operations, security, and scalability.

What is your primary technology stack?

We are framework-agnostic but heavily favor modern, battle-tested technologies. For the frontend, we use React, Next.js, and Astro. For backend APIs, we rely on Node.js and scalable databases like PostgreSQL and MongoDB. For mobile, we build native-feeling apps using React Native and Flutter.

Do you work with startups or established enterprises?

Both. We help founders build scalable MVPs from scratch, and we assist established enterprises in modernizing legacy systems, migrating to the cloud, and implementing digital transformation strategies.

How do you handle project pricing and scoping?

We offer multiple engagement models. For well-defined scopes, we provide fixed-price contracts with clear milestones. For ongoing development or scaling SaaS platforms, we offer dedicated team retainers. We usually start with a paid discovery phase to map out the exact requirements and select the best model.
